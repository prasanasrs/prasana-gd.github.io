About Me

BUILDING
WORLDS FROM
IMAGINATION

I'm Prasana Vengatesh, a passionate game designer with hands-on experience in Unreal Engine and Blender. My work spans action, survival, and simulation genres — every project a chance to push the boundaries of what a game can feel like.

I believe great games are built on three pillars: compelling mechanics, immersive environments, and a story that the player lives rather than watches. From AI-driven enemies in a maze to the quiet tension of island survival, my games put the player at the center.

"Games are the only art form where the audience is also the protagonist."
